Active Design Physical Activity and Health in
Flushing - Clearview

The design and conditions of buildings, streets, public transportation and parks influence physical activity, use of active transportation and other healthy behavior. A neighborhood's features can also impact the safety of its residents.

Flushing - Clearview includes ZIP Codes: 11351,11354,11355,11356,11357,11358,11359,11360,11368.

Injury and Health

Neighborhood design can influence health both directly -- by way of immediate hazards in the environment, and indirectly, for example, by encouraging exercise.

What is the City doing?
  • Publishes Active Design Guidelines to guide ongoing development.
  • Builds safer streets for all New Yorkers.
  • Plants street trees, manages sanitation, and maintains parks.
What can you do?
  • Get physical activity - there are many health benefits.
  • Call 311 to report problems with sidewalks, parks, or other parts of your neighborhood.
  • Request a street tree for your neighborhood.

Indicator ranking

Each indicator is placed in one of three categories:

  • Better
  • Middle
  • Worse

This shows us how the neighborhood compares to other NYC neighborhoods, if you ranked all of the NYC neighborhoods based on this indicator.

  • Better: in the top 1/3rd of all NYC neighorhoods.
  • Middle: it's close to the NYC median - ranking somewhere in the middle third of all neighborhoods.
  • Worse: in the bottom 1/3rd of NYC neighborhoods.

These are only based on the distribution of data values, not the range. The absolute values of indicators in different categories may not be all that different.
